Case Study: Payroll HRIS Insourcing

Agility, Reliability, and Cost Reduction.

The Context

Payroll management for the 5 entities of the "A Lundi" group was outsourced, leading to high costs, a lack of flexibility, and difficulties in managing the complexity of multiple collective bargaining agreements. The objective was to bring this critical function in-house.

My Role

As a Payroll Manager and Deployment Project Manager, I led the entire migration and insourcing project.

My Approach

  • Deployment Project Management: I managed the implementation of the MADPER (Tetra Informatique) software for a scope of 250 monthly payrolls.
  • Advanced Functional Configuration: I carried out the complete configuration of the tool, including the 5 structures, company files, and especially the complex payroll rules (bonuses, allowances) and the specific module for invoicing hours.
  • Stabilization and Launch: To secure the transition, I organized a parallel run payroll phase for several months to fully validate the configurations. I then managed the go-live and the first complete cycle independently.

Results

  • Increased agility in HR management (reduced final settlement times, responsiveness to variables).
  • Drastically **simplified and secured** the invoicing process.
  • Significant **reduction in processing costs** compared to outsourcing.
